Case Study

Operational Organization & CRM Support

Company
NexusCloud IT Solutions Inc.
Industry
IT Training — CCNA, Cloud, Cybersecurity
Role
General Virtual Assistant
Focus
Operations, Data Management, CRM

Objective

Support daily operations by organizing scattered Excel-based records, improving lead tracking, maintaining accurate student and payment data, and ensuring consistent administrative workflows.

Business Challenge

The company relied on manual Excel sheets and fragmented communication tools. This caused:

  • Disorganized student and lead records
  • Missed and inconsistent follow-ups
  • Scheduling conflicts between students and instructors
  • Unclear tracking of partial payments and balances

Outcomes

  • Organized and centralized all operational records
  • More consistent lead follow-up process
  • Reduced scheduling conflicts through structured tracking
  • Improved visibility of payment status and balances
  • Clearer student and lead lifecycle tracking

Key Workflows

1 — Data Organization Consolidated multiple Excel files, cleaned duplicates, standardized formatting across students, leads, courses, and payments.
2 — Lead Tracking & Follow-up Logged Facebook and referral leads. Executed Day 1 → Day 3–5 → Day 7 follow-up schedule via SMS, email, and call.
3 — Scheduling Coordination Managed enrollment schedules, coordinated instructor availability, and prevented conflicts before confirming classes.
4 — Payment Tracking Maintained accounts receivable sheet. Logged installments, verified receipts, updated overdue balances, and sent reminders.

Tools Used

Microsoft Excel Google Sheets CRM System Gmail / Outlook Google Calendar SMS Tools

Value delivered: Clean data, structured workflows, and consistent follow-ups — allowing the business to reduce administrative errors and improve operational reliability without adding more headcount.
